How Pomodoro Works

01
Pick a Task

Focus on one single goal at a time. Multi-tasking is the enemy of deep work.

02
Start Timer

Set the clock to 25 minutes. Immerse yourself completely until the alarm rings.

03
Take a Breath

Take a 5-minute break to recharge. Stretch, move, or grab some water.

04
Deep Reset

After 4 sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break to fully reset your mind.
